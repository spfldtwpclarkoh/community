Community & Safety, Hand in Hand

The Springfield Township Fire Department is dedicated to protecting our community not just by responding to emergencies, but by preventing them. Explore our outreach programs designed to keep you and your family safe.

Explore Our Programs

Our Mission in the Community

Our Community Outreach initiative is built on the belief that a well-informed community is a safer community. We strive to build strong relationships with residents and businesses through education, prevention programs, and active participation in local events.

Our Community Outreach Services

We offer a variety of free programs and services to the residents of Springfield Township.

Fire Safety Education

Presentations for schools, community groups, and businesses on fire prevention, escape planning, and fire extinguisher use.

CPR & First Aid Classes

Certified training courses for individuals and groups. Learn life-saving skills from the professionals.

Smoke Detector Program

We provide and install free smoke detectors for residents in need, ensuring every home is protected.

Fire Station Tours

Schedule a visit to our station! Meet the firefighters, see the trucks up close, and learn about our equipment.

Community Event Visits

Request a fire engine and crew to visit your block party, festival, or other local event. Subject to availability.

Business Safety Surveys

We offer complimentary fire safety walkthroughs for local businesses to help identify and mitigate potential hazards.
